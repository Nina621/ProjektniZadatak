Berners-Lee is British computer scientist who invented the WWW.

Born on 8 June 1955 and grew up in London. He studied physics at Oxford University and became a softwer engineer.

In 1980 he first described the concept of global system, based on the concept of "hypertext" that would allow research anywhere to share information.He aloso built a prototype called "Enquire".

1984 Berners-Lee returned to CERN, which also home to a major Europian Internet node. In 1989 published a paper calledd "Information Managment: A Proposal" in which he married up hypertext with the Internet, to create a system for sharing and distributing information not just within a company, but globally. He named it the World Wide Web.

He also creadted first web browser and editor. It explained the World Wide Web concept and gave users an introduction to getting started with their own websites.

In 1994, Berners-Lee founded the World WIde Web Consortium at the Massachusetts Institute of Tehnology in Boston. He has served as director of the consortium since then. He also works as a senior reasrch scientist at LCS which has now become the Computer Science and Artificial Inteligence Laboratory.
